who am i?

I'm a 23 year old Graphic Designer based in Liverpool, England.
I love to design clean, simple & beautiful interfaces for web & mobile devices.
I also spend time playing around with shapes, lines and colours.

Friday Beers

This project was to create a fun, attention-grabbing poster to promote a new internal communications event being planned by a leading digital agency. As the project was for internal use, this allowed for me to have a bit of fun with the design.

Pizza Slice

I decided to do a quick 15 min illustration of one of my favourite things to chow down on. I kept the strokes to 10px throughout this illo, allowing for a pretty clean & really simple design. Besides, who doesn't like a slice of 'za?

storiel

Storiel is a museum & art gallery located in Bangor, Wales. As this was one of the first clients I was working for with Rippleffect, my creative director suggested it may be a good idea for me to design a pitch (although we had already secured this client) to gain an understanding about design process, budgets, and following their brand guidelines.

Visit canada

This is a mobile application I made in my spare time to familiarise myself with design principles when designing for mobile devices. The idea behind this application is to feed relevant content to tourists when visiting a user specified location such as where to eat, what to do, or where to stay, in hopes that it would boost local communities, and small businesses.
